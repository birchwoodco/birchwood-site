How We Work

Most marketing conversations start with what people see first — a new website, a new campaign, new materials. Each one built on a story that was never properly articulated.

Birchwood works the other direction. We start from the inside first: who you actually are, what you actually believe, and where your message and your reality have drifted apart. We believe the most powerful story emerges from that discovery, and we build everything from there.

The Work

Three phases. One continuous thread.

Identity first. Story second. Expression third.

01

Identity

Who you actually are.

It might seem odd to start here. But, over time, organizational mission can shift and drift. The result is a team that no longer knows how to talk about itself. Bandaid fixes like new campaigns, new branding, and better content won't solve this at the root.

We enter at the beginning. Through facilitated sessions with your leadership team, we close the gap between your mission and how you've been describing it, and produce a story framework your entire organization can speak from. The work is internal before it is ever external.

02

Story

How you say what's true.

Knowing who you are and being able to articulate it are two different things. Most organizations have the identity. What they're missing is how to tie the heart of who they are with language that actually works — positioning, messaging architecture, and a throughline their team can use.

We identify your target audience and how to best reach them. We take what was found in the Identity phase and translate it into words that work. The result is a story framework your team can speak from without us in the room.

03

Expression

Your story, brought to life.

Once the foundation is in place, we build. Birchwood and its network of specialists — documentary filmmakers, brand designers, and content strategists, each working at the highest level of their craft — translate your story into the content and media your organization needs to reach your audience effectively. Because the foundation work came first, everything that follows has something real to say.

  • Films and documentary video — organizational story films, campaign and fundraising video, short-form social content, built to award-winning documentary standards
  • Content strategy and creation — editorial voice, website copy, and ongoing content your team can sustain
  • Ongoing partnership and retainer — a consistent strategic voice and content partner over time: monthly strategy, content development, campaign support, and as-needed counsel
  • Trusted partners — for visual identity, brand design, and logo systems, Birchwood connects you with vetted specialists who do this work at the highest level

About Birchwood

Faith-rooted imagination. Clarity without reduction.

She learned what it takes to make a story reach people at the highest level. And she kept watching mission-driven organizations — the ones doing work that actually matters — pour money into websites and campaigns built on foundations that were never properly laid.

Birchwood exists because of that gap. Not between organizations and their audiences, but between who organizations actually are and how they've been showing up in the world. Valerie built a practice around closing it, inside-out, every time.
