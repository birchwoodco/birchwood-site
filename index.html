About Birchwood

We are creative partners who believe the most important work an organization can do is understand who it actually is and learn to communicate that with integrity. We ask questions most strategists won't, sit with complexity most processes skip, and help you find what's real before we tell you how to say it.

Most communications work starts outside and moves in. We work the other direction. Identity first. Story second. Expression third.

We sit with organizations at moments of tension, transition, and transformation. Not to hand them a framework, but to do the slow work of listening that makes the right framework possible. The story is always already there. The work is drawing it out.

We are especially at home with mission-driven, faith-informed, or organizations focused on social good. These are contexts where story carries weight that goes beyond marketing.

Strategy With Soul

If your team gives different answers about who you are, if your messaging feels off, if you've invested in campaigns that didn't land the way they should, that's not a marketing problem. It's a story problem. It starts somewhere specific, and it's fixable.

Plenty of agencies will grow your impressions. They'll build a website, run a campaign, produce a video. But if the story underneath doesn't reflect the actual depth and uniqueness of your organization, none of it sticks. You end up with better packaging around a message that was never properly found. We start underneath.

How We Work

Every engagement follows three movements, but where you enter depends on what you need. The sequence doesn't change.

01

Identity

Who you actually are.

Most organizations have never had a room where someone asked the real questions. Through facilitated sessions with your stakeholders, we surface the foundation your organization is actually built on. When it's done, your team is aligned and can speak from that foundation with confidence.

02

Story

How you say it.

Once we know what's real, we build the language that carries it. A story framework your team can speak from without us in the room. Messaging that holds together across every channel and context because it all originates from the same place.

03

Expression

What you put into the world.

We produce films, campaigns, and content built on what we've uncovered together. The difference is that ours originates from what is distinctly yours. Our network of documentary filmmakers, content strategists, writers, and creatives translate your story into content that reaches your audience.

  • Films and documentary video
  • Content strategy and creation
  • Ongoing partnership and retainer
  • Trusted partners for visual identity and brand design

Founded by

Valerie McGowan spent over a decade inside two worlds that rarely speak to each other: the highest levels of the entertainment industry, and the ground-level work of mission-driven organizations. In entertainment, she learned that the difference between a story that moves people and one that doesn't is almost never the budget. It's the clarity underneath it.

Prior to entering social impact entertainment, she worked in nonprofit community development, where she learned how to earn trust inside organizations doing work that matters, and how to help them reach beyond their traditional boundaries without losing their core identity. Birchwood is where those two things meet.
